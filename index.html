Q&A

Got questions about SkillBridge? We have answers to help you post your first task or offer your first skill with confidence.

  • Post a task describing what you need, set a budget or swap offer, and we match you with nearby helpers who have the right skill. Accept the best fit and get it done — fast.

  • Anything from spreadsheet fixes and design work to tutoring, video editing, handyman help, and career coaching. If it is a real skill, someone nearby likely has it.

  • You can offer a cash rate, propose a skill exchange, offer a reward, or request free community help. All arrangements are agreed between you and your helper before work begins.

  • Browsing and posting tasks is always free. SkillBridge earns a small service fee only when a paid task is successfully completed — no upfront cost to try.
